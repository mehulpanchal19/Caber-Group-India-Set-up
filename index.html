Operating Model & Regional Support

The India entity will operate strictly as an internal delivery centre, with regional entities retaining all client accountability and professional responsibility.

In Scope

  • Civil and structural drafting
  • Transport infrastructure design support
  • Digital engineering and BIM modelling
  • CAD production and design documentation
  • Drawing checks and QA support
  • Data, quantity take-offs, and modelling support

Out of Scope

  • Client contracting or revenue billing
  • Design sign-off or statutory certification
  • Site supervision or construction roles
  • Independent advice or authority engineer roles

Governance Principle

Support-only model.

All client accountability, professional indemnity exposure, and contractual responsibility remain with regional operating entities.
